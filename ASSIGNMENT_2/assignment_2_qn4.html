Nature's Wonders

Mountains

Mountains are a beautiful part of nature. Many people love to visit them for hiking and adventure. The air is fresh, and the view from the top is amazing. Some mountains are covered in snow, while others have green forests. Mountains are also home to many wild animals.

Oceans

Oceans are huge and cover most of the Earth. They are full of different sea animals, like fish, sharks, and whales. Many people visit the ocean to swim and relax at the beach. The waves move because of the wind, and the water is salty.

Forests

Forests are full of trees, plants, and animals. They give us fresh air and help keep the Earth cool. Many animals, like deer, tigers, and birds, live in forests. Trees make the oxygen that we breathe, so we must protect forests to keep our planet healthy.
